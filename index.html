    Welcome to Sportnaa Football Agency! Located in Bahrain , we are the pioneering agency in the region that is dedicated to enhancing Bahraini football. Our goal is to support and represent Talented young players, providing them with opportunities to showcase their skills and reach their full potential.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to fulfill the ambitious goal of His Highness Sheikh Nasser Bin Hamad by empowering Bahraini football and realizing the Bahraini Dream (World Cup 2030) through strategic initiatives, including facilitating the placement of Bahraini players in European Clubs, conducting Tryout Programs in Europe, implementing Exchange programs for Bahraini coaches, organizing impactful football Events, and recognizing the Talents of our Youth players through prestigious awards.

    Player’Naa Award
    Sami Bassam was chosen for the Player'Naa Award

    Sportnaa introduced the Player’naa Award, recognizing the Best Player in the Kingdom of Bahrain for varies age groups. This Award is part of the process of honoring the outstanding talents of Bahraini Footballers who excelled in the calendar year.
